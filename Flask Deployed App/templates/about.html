🌿 About PlantGuard AI

An intelligent plant disease detection platform that empowers farmers and gardeners with the power of deep learning — instantly identify crop diseases and take action.

🎯 Our Mission

PlantGuard AI was built to solve one of agriculture's biggest challenges — early and accurate plant disease identification. By combining convolutional neural networks trained on thousands of plant images, we give farmers a fast and reliable tool to diagnose diseases right from their smartphone, without any expert knowledge required.

🔬 How the Technology Works

Our AI engine uses a deep convolutional neural network (CNN) trained on the PlantVillage dataset covering 39 different plant disease classes. When you upload a photo of a leaf, the model analyzes visual features like color patterns, texture irregularities, and lesions to classify the disease with high accuracy — all in real time.
